# RapidMC - 1.20.4
## Features

**Performance Optimization**

**Lightning Speed:** Carefully selected performance mods like Sodium and Lithium ensure your game runs faster than ever. Enjoy improved fluidity, even on modest devices.

**OptiFine Parity:** Rapid MC provides support for most OptiFine features, including shaders, connected textures, and zoom. If a resource pack requires “OptiFine,” it’s likely to work here too. You can even create your custom cape!
Conveniences. **Thanks to the FO (Fabulously Optimized) optimization mod**

**Familiar Experience:** Rapid MC lets you relive Minecraft as you remember it, regardless of the version you’ve played before. Use game controllers, play in local worlds with friends, check advancements in a large window, and more.

**Universal Compatibility:** Since Rapid MC doesn’t include content mods, you can play on any world or server. While you can use any game launcher, we recommend those that offer the best experience.
Constant Updates

**Always Up-to-Date:** Rapid MC updates swiftly and is one of the first modpacks to adopt new Minecraft versions. Updating the pack is straightforward and preserves your mod configurations whenever possible.
Based on Fabric

**Lightweight and Versatile:** Rapid MC uses the Fabric mod loader, known for its lightweight nature and compatibility with a wide variety of mods. Use it as a base to add your favorite mods.
